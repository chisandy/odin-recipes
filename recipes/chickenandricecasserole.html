CHICKEN AND RICE CASSEROLE

DESCRIPTION

This recipe is so easy to follow that one need not have cooking skills to be able to pull off such meal.

Cooking and preping takes about 30-45 minnutes . Although most people would be weiry of its simply nature but i assure you that this meal is a goldmine.

INGEDIENTS

  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 7/8 cups of water
  • 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Spread rice in the bottom of a 9x13 inche baking dish. Rinse chicken and pat dry; arrange chicken pieces on top of rice.
  3. Mix soup and water together and pour over chicken and rice. Sprinkle dry onion soup mix on top. Cover and seal TIGHTLY with foil. Bake in the preheated oven for 1hour 30minutes.
